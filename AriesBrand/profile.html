roberto simioni

lead technisian

As a Mechanical Lead at HAX Shenzhen, Charles is responsible for supporting HAX teams with their mechanical development over the course of the accelerator program and ensure that the team's mechanical designs are production-ready and up to industrial standards.

Before joining HAX, Charles has worked at Navyug Security as R&D Head for Robotics, where he led a team of engineers on the design and production of robotic vehicles and simulation systems, and at Motherson Techno Tools as a Lead Design Engineer, designing precision CNC tools, fixtures, and development of tooling for new products.

Charles is a Mechanical Engineer by training and has expertise in CAE design with vast experience in Machining, conventional production methods, and industrial standards. He has a firm grasp of multiphysics, dynamic, and nonlinear systems and a functional understanding of electronic and embedded systems.
